Region Rules

Regions marked as protected are covered by common law. This means the mods can enforce server rules in these areas.

Regions marked as unprotected are NOT covered by common law. This means the mods CANNOT enforce server rules in these areas.

Claimed areas may carry additional Rules which will be updated on the map page as necessary
