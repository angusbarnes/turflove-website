TurfLove: Legacy

The Culmination of 12 Seasons and almost a decade of SMP.

TurfLove has a rich history with its original incarnation, TurfWar, taking place in early 2017. Since then, the series has seen many iterations including, but not limited to:

  • TurfLove: Survival Island
  • TurfLove: To The Skies
  • TurfLove: Evolved (Modded)
  • CommuCraft
  • Politicraft
  • Unknown SMP
    •

    Over the years we have had many players, many worlds and many great builds. TurfLove Legacy has been a project of mine for some time now with the help of a senior member I have managed to successfully transplant many of the most noticeable builds into one final world. This is not just another 'series' - its a fullscale project and we need your help.

    The most dedicated community members will be given access to the Legacy SMP with the goal of finishing off many old builds and projects and continuing the legacy.
